Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to make an appointment?

While we do accept walk-ins when possible, we recommend booking an appointment to ensure you get your preferred time slot with your barber of choice. You can book online, by phone, or in-person.

How often should I get a haircut?

This depends on your hairstyle and how quickly your hair grows. On average, we recommend every 3-4 weeks for short styles and every 6-8 weeks for longer styles. For beard trims, every 2-3 weeks helps maintain your ideal shape.

What services do you offer?

We offer a full range of services including classic haircuts, modern styles, beard trims, hot towel shaves, hair coloring, and scalp treatments.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit/debit cards, and mobile payment methods including Apple Pay and Google Pay.

Do you have a cancellation policy?

We request at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows may result in a cancellation fee for future bookings. We understand emergencies happen, so please just let us know as soon as possible.
