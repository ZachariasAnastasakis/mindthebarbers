Our Story

How We Started

In the heart of Vyronas, Athens, where tradition meets modern style, a small barbershop called "Mind The Barbers" opened its doors. Founded by the barber Sotiris Kamperis, this haven of masculine grooming quickly became more than just a place for haircuts—it became a community.

The shop on Mesologgiou 5 started with a simple philosophy: every man deserves exceptional grooming delivered with genuine care. Using premium products like Uppercut, Reuzel, and Proraso, the skilled barbers transform each visit into an experience. From classic cuts to traditional straight-razor shaves, their expertise has earned them recognition as one of the top barbershops in Athens.

What truly sets Mind The Barbers apart isn't just technical skill—it's the warm atmosphere where stories are shared, friendships form, and men leave feeling not just well-groomed, but rejuvenated. Step inside our doors, and you'll understand why our clients return time after time. At Mind The Barbers, we believe grooming isn't just a service—it's an art form that honors the gentleman in every man.

Our Mission

"To provide exceptional grooming services that help men look their best, feel confident, and enjoy a moment of relaxation in their busy lives."

At Mind The Barbers, we believe that a great haircut is more than just a service - it's an experience. We're committed to maintaining the time-honored traditions of barbering while embracing innovation and contemporary style.
